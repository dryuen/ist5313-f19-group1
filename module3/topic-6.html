Americano Fun Facts

Did you know??

  • It is rumored that it's name was first used during World War II.
  • After the falling out between the US and Russia, the Russian Prime Minister proposed for this type of coffee to be renamed to Russiano. Although the proposal was humorous, some cafes in Moscow did implement that name.
  • There is a cocktail named The Americano, so make sure when ordering you specify an Americano Coffee.
  • Baristas love when you order an Americano, why? Because it is the easiest beverage to make in their entire menu. It's simply a diluted espresso shot.
