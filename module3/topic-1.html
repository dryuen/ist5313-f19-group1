Introduction

Even if you may not know any fancy Italian terms for coffees, it's a fair bet that you may have gone into a coffee shop sometime in the last 15 or so years, which means you have encountered the Americano in some form. Many coffee shop menus use the term Americano as a fancy term that really means standard black coffee. So where did this delicious drink originate from? How has it evolved over time? All those questions and much more will be answered for you in this module, from its recipe to fun facts, to where to find it locally in San Antonio.
