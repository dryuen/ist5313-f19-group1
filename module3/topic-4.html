Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • Boiling water
  • Optional: Steamed milk
  • Optional: Sugar

Steps

  1. Make a shot of espresso coffee and pour it into a 6-ounce cup.

  2. Add boiling water into the cup until the coffee reaches the top.

  3. 3. Have steamed milk and sugar on the side to add.

  4. Enjoy!
