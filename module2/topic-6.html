Cappuccino Fun Facts

Did you know??

  • The term cappuccino stems from a religious order: The Capuchin Friars. It is rumored that it was named after the friar Marco D'aviano, who led the Turkish siege of Vienna in 1683. However its not yet been confirmed by historical evidence.
  • Cappuccinos are one of the most difficult types of coffees to prepare properly because of the close attention needed in the foam ratio when steaming the milk.
  • National Cappuccino Day is November 8th
  • The top foam layer in your cappuccino is usually where you will encounter the strong flavors that give your drink that special taste (i.e. cinnamon, ginger, cocoa)
