Altering to Dietary Needs

Below are items that can be substituted in the recipe to ensure that all dietary needs are met.

  • Decaf espresso

Sugar

  • Honey
  • Agave
  • Stevia
  • Sugar Free Flavored Syrups

Milk

  • Half and Half
  • Soy Milk
  • Almond Milk
  • Coconut Milk
