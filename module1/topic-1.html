Introduction

Can I get a grande vanilla latte with extra vanilla, no whip. Oh, and make that iced please. This is the common plea we hear when walking into a Starbucks. And why wouldn't we, who doesn't love a creamy cup of freshly brewed Latte? It's one of the most popular types of coffee drinks (out of many) that is enjoyed all over the world. And although the term latte is Italian and that may make you think it originated in Italy, it actually is not! In this module you will learn the history of a latte, how it has evolved over time, and an easy to follow recipe for you to enjoy a homemade latte from the comfort of your home.You will also learn some fun facts about the latte and where to find it locally in San Antonio.
