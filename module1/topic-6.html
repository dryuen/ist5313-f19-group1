Latte Fun Facts

Did you know??

  • The term latte is italian, meaning milk. So if you were to walk into an italian coffee shop and asked for a latte you would receive a plain glass of milk! When ordering you have to say caffe latte to receive this coffee beverage. And when visiting a spanish-speaking nation when ordering a latte you ask for cafe con leche.
  • October 7th is National Latte Day
  • Latte art is all about the science of milk and legend has it that David Schomer is the man behind it all whom started the US latte art craze in the mid 1980s.
  • Many cafes around the world offer latte art trainings and there are even latte art championships worldwide!
