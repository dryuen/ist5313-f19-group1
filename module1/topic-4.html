Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 2 shots foamed milk
  • Optional: 1 shot of flavored simple syrup

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients

  2. Pour the espresso shot into your latte cup and add a shot of flavored syrup is desired.

  3. Using a spoon, retain the microbubbles on top of the steamed milk, pour the bottom 2/3 of the steamed milk from the steaming pitcher into the latte cup.

  4. Top the latte with the remaining bubbles.

  5. Enjoy!
